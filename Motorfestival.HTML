Motorfestival in Poland

By Tom De Kroon

Every year in Łagów, there is a motorfestival.

The festival lasts for 4 days, and motorcycle riders from all over Europe come to show and see other bikes.

During the event, motorcycle riders from Europe meet new people, show or even sell their bikes, and some even try to race each other.

What is there to do?

There are many food trucks and other stands where you can buy various items.

There is a big stage where bands play every night.

On the festival grounds, there is a large campsite where you can stay for the duration of the event.

On Saturday, there is a big parade where all the riders drive through the village.

If you like bikes and cars, this is the place to be!

Staying here

But be careful. Many places raise their prices during this event, and some are even closed.

If you want to attend this event, the date is usually the second weekend of July.

“Once a year, the road calls louder than life itself.”
